Mint Chicken

Description

Mint Chicken is a flavorful and aromatic dish that combines tender chicken with the refreshing taste of mint. Marinated in a blend of fresh mint leaves, yogurt, garlic, ginger, and warm spices, the chicken is then grilled, baked, or stir-fried to perfection. This dish offers a perfect balance of savory, tangy, and herbaceous flavors, making it a delicious choice for a light yet satisfying meal. Serve it with rice, naan, or a fresh salad for a refreshing and wholesome dining experience!

Ingredients:

For the Marinade:

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup fresh mint leaves (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup fresh cilantro (finely chopped)
  • ½ cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp oil

For Cooking:

  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 1 small onion (finely sliced, optional)
  • 1 green chili (sliced, optional for extra spice)
