Lasanga

Description:

Lasanga is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and gooey melted cheese on top. Baked to perfection, this hearty dish boasts a perfect balance of flavors—savory, cheesy, and slightly tangy from the tomato sauce. Whether made with traditional Bolognese, spinach and ricotta, or a plant-based filling, lasagna is a comforting meal enjoyed worldwide. Serve it hot with a side of garlic bread and a fresh salad for a complete, satisfying feast!

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tsp dried)
  • ¼ tsp salt

Other Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (cooked and drained)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)

Optional Additions:

  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (for extra spice)
  • 1 cup fresh spinach (for a veggie twist)
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms (for added texture)
